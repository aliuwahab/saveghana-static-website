About SAVE-Ghana

SAVE-Ghana is a Ghanaian NGO founded in 2004 and legally incorporated in 2008. What started as a Youth Action Club led by enthusiastic high school graduates has transformed into a vibrant and proactive local NGO operating across Northern Ghana.

We work with rural and deprived communities, focusing on promoting quality delivery of basic social services including education, health, women empowerment, sustainable livelihoods, and safeguarding the rights of women and children.
